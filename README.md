LinkFlow — Chrome Extension
Extract any URLs from messy text and launch them all in multiple tabs with a single click.

How to Use
Copy any content containing links (from Google Docs, Slack, emails, or briefs)

Click the LinkFlow icon in your Chrome toolbar

Click the Paste button to import your content

Click Extract & Open to launch all unique links into new tabs instantly

Features
Intelligent Extraction: Deep-scans text to identify all HTTP and HTTPS links.

De-duplication: Automatically filters out duplicate URLs to keep your workspace clean.

Batch Launching: Opens dozens of links simultaneously without slowing down your browser.

Privacy-Centric: No data collection—all processing happens locally on your machine.

Requirements
Google Chrome or any Chromium-based browser.
